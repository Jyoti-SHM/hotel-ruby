Hotel Ruby

Tucked away on a peaceful lane off the main road, our heritage-style hotel offers a perfect blend of traditional Rajasthani charm and modern comforts.​

Just 1.5 km from Jaipur Junction, Hotel Ruby provides easy access to the city's vibrant markets and iconic landmarks like Hawa Mahal and City Palace. Our 49 well-appointed deluxe rooms are designed to ensure a comfortable and memorable stay.​​

Experience the warmth of Rajasthani hospitality at Hotel Ruby—where tradition meets comfort.
